# Daily Rush - Video Tutorial Script

## Video Structure (5-7 minutes)

---

## **1. INTRODUCTION (30 seconds)**

### What to say:
"Hey everyone! Welcome to Daily Rush - your all-in-one productivity companion. In this video, I'll show you how to use this powerful task management and focus timer app to boost your productivity and stay organized. Let's get started!"

### What to show:
- Open the website in browser
- Show the homepage with login prompt
- Highlight the clean, modern design

---

## **2. GETTING STARTED - SIGN UP/LOGIN (1 minute)**

### What to say:
"First things first, you'll need to create an account. Daily Rush offers two ways to sign in - you can either use your Google account for quick access, or create an account with your email and password."

### What to do:
1. Click "Sign In" button
2. Show the login modal with two tabs: "Sign In" and "Sign Up"
3. **Option 1 - Google Sign-In:**
   - Click "Continue with Google"
   - Show Google authentication popup
   - "This is the fastest way to get started!"
   
4. **Option 2 - Email/Password:**
   - Switch to "Sign Up" tab
   - Fill in: Email, Password (min 6 characters), Optional Username
   - Click "Sign Up"
   - "Make sure to use a strong password!"

### What to highlight:
- Clean, user-friendly authentication interface
- Both methods are secure and work seamlessly

---

## **3. MAIN FEATURES OVERVIEW (30 seconds)**

### What to say:
"Once you're logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard. Daily Rush has four main sections: All Tasks, Active Tasks, Completed Tasks, and the Focus Timer. Let me show you each one."

### What to show:
- Point to the navigation buttons
- Show the points display in the header
- Show user profile area

---

## **4. CREATING AND MANAGING TASKS (2 minutes)**

### What to say:
"Let's start by creating your first task. This is where the magic happens!"

### What to do:

**Creating a Task:**
1. In the task input area, type a task name
   - Example: "Complete project presentation"
2. **Select Difficulty Level:**
   - Easy (10 points)
   - Medium (25 points) - default
   - Hard (50 points)
   - Expert (100 points)
   - "The harder the task, the more points you earn when you complete it!"
3. **Set Due Date (Optional):**
   - Click the date picker
   - "You can assign a due date to stay on track"
   - Select a date
4. Click "Add Task"
5. "Your task appears immediately in your list!"

### What to highlight:
- Modern card-based design
- All options are optional except the task name
- Points system is gamified for motivation

**Task Features:**
- Show task with difficulty badge (color-coded)
- Show date badge if date is set:
  - Red = Overdue
  - Orange = Today
  - Blue = Upcoming (within 7 days)
  - Gray = Future
- Click checkbox to complete task
- "Watch the points add up!"
- Show the points notification animation
- "You can delete tasks by clicking the delete button"

**Task Organization:**
1. Switch between tabs:
   - **All Tasks**: See everything
   - **Active Tasks**: Only incomplete tasks
   - **Completed Tasks**: Your accomplishments
2. "Each view has search and sort functionality"

---

## **5. POINTS SYSTEM & GAMIFICATION (1 minute)**

### What to say:
"Daily Rush uses a gamified points system to keep you motivated. Let's see how it works!"

### What to show:
1. Point to the points display in header
2. Complete a task:
   - Easy task = +10 points
   - Medium task = +25 points
   - Hard task = +50 points
   - Expert task = +100 points
3. Show the animated notification
4. "Points are tracked across all your tasks and give you a sense of achievement!"

---

## **6. POMODORO FOCUS TIMER (2 minutes)**

### What to say:
"This is one of my favorite features - the Pomodoro Focus Timer. It helps you maintain deep focus and complete tasks more efficiently."

### What to do:

**Navigating to Timer:**
1. Click "Focus" in navigation
2. "Here's your personal focus station!"

**Timer Features:**
1. **Modes:**
   - **Work (25 min)**: For focused work sessions
   - **Short Break (5 min)**: Quick rest between sessions
   - **Long Break (15 min)**: After 4 work sessions
   - Click each mode button
   
2. **Select a Task to Focus On:**
   - Open task dropdown
   - "You can choose a specific task to work on during your focus session"
   - Select a task
   - "The task is displayed so you stay focused"

3. **Using the Timer:**
   - Click "Start" button
   - "Watch the circular progress indicator - it's color-coded by mode"
   - Show the countdown
   - "You can pause if needed, or reset to start over"
   - "When you complete a full 25-minute work session, you get 10 bonus points!"
   - Wait for timer to complete (or fast-forward)
   - Show completion notification
   - Show bonus points notification
   - "Notice how it automatically switches to break mode"

4. **Statistics:**
   - Point to "Pomodoros Today" counter
   - Point to "Total Focus Time" counter
   - "These stats help you track your productivity over time"

### What to highlight:
- Beautiful circular timer design
- Smooth animations
- Automatic mode switching
- Bonus points reward system
- Visual and audio feedback

---

## **7. TASK MANAGEMENT FEATURES (1 minute)**

### What to say:
"Let me show you some powerful organization features."

### What to show:

**Search Functionality:**
1. Go to any task view
2. Type in search box
3. "Search filters tasks in real-time"

**Sorting:**
1. Show sort dropdown
2. Options:
   - Recently Added / Oldest First
   - A-Z / Z-A
   - Recently Completed / Oldest Completed
3. "Organize tasks exactly how you want"

**Stats Cards:**
- Point to active tasks count
- Point to completed tasks count
- "Quick overview of your progress"

**Motivation Messages:**
- Show motivational text that appears
- "The app encourages you along the way!"

---

## **8. USER PROFILE (30 seconds)**

### What to say:
"You can customize your profile to make it yours."

### What to show:
1. Click on profile area
2. Show edit profile option
3. "You can customize your name and profile picture"
4. "All your data syncs across devices when logged in"

---

## **9. KEY TIPS & BEST PRACTICES (30 seconds)**

### What to say:
"Here are some pro tips to get the most out of Daily Rush:"

### Tips to mention:
1. "Use difficulty levels honestly - they help track your real progress"
2. "Set due dates for important tasks to never miss deadlines"
3. "Complete Pomodoro sessions for bonus points and better focus"
4. "Regularly review your completed tasks - it's motivating!"
5. "Use the search function when you have many tasks"
6. "Take breaks seriously - the Pomodoro timer reminds you"

---

## **10. CLOSING (30 seconds)**

### What to say:
"And that's Daily Rush! A powerful, beautiful productivity tool that combines task management with the proven Pomodoro technique. 

Remember:
- Create tasks with difficulty levels
- Set due dates to stay organized
- Use the Focus Timer for deep work
- Earn points and bonuses for motivation
- Track your progress with statistics

Try it out and let me know what you think! If you have questions, feel free to reach out. Thanks for watching, and happy productivity!"

### What to show:
- Final overview of the app
- Show points counter
- Show completed tasks
- Show Pomodoro stats
- Close with logo/homepage

---

## **BONUS: QUICK TIPS FOR RECORDING**

### Technical Tips:
1. **Screen Recording:**
   - Use OBS Studio, Camtasia, or built-in screen recorder
   - Record in 1080p minimum
   - Show browser at 80-100% zoom

2. **Audio:**
   - Use a good microphone
   - Record in quiet environment
   - Test audio levels first

3. **Editing:**
   - Add text overlays for key features
   - Highlight buttons with arrows/circles
   - Use zoom-ins for important details
   - Add smooth transitions

4. **Pacing:**
   - Speak clearly and at moderate pace
   - Pause briefly when showing UI elements
   - Don't rush through features

5. **Visual Aids:**
   - Use cursor highlighting (bigger cursor)
   - Add callout boxes in editing
   - Use slow-motion for animations
   - Add annotations for bonus tips

### Demo Data Setup:
Before recording, create some sample data:
- 3-4 tasks with different difficulty levels
- Some tasks with dates (today, tomorrow, overdue)
- Mix of completed and active tasks
- At least 1 completed Pomodoro session
